About IPAS

Initiative for Peace and Stability (IPAS) is a non-governmental organization committed to the prevention of Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV), Violence Against Women and Girls (VAWG), and all forms of abuse that threaten peace, dignity, and human rights.

IPAS works through advocacy, confidential reporting, counseling support, mentorship, and community engagement to promote justice, healing, and long-term social stability.

Our Vision

A society where women, girls, and vulnerable persons live free from violence in a culture of sustainable peace and mutual respect.

Our Mission

To prevent and respond to gender-based violence through education, advocacy, survivor support, and peace-building initiatives.

About the Founder

Mrs. Joy Isemede is the founder and owner of Initiative for Peace and Stability (IPAS).

She is a mother of four beautiful girls and hails from Edo State, Nigeria. Mrs. Isemede is a trained counselor, a mentor to many, and a minister with a deep passion for guiding individuals toward healing, purpose, and emotional well-being.

Her work is driven by a strong belief in sustainable peace and the conviction that addressing gender-based violence is essential to building stable families and communities.
